Here, the arbitrary has met a moment.
Here, the flesh has been enough.

The vacancy was already articulated in all its variations.
Language which seeks clarity but resists illumination.

The form is pulling against the sensation. The matter has ceased to function as it had,
having been vibrated to a point of discordance. The strategy, an unknown guest in the house.

The answer as a call, oscillating among tender and callous
so quickly it appears to be whole.

Material Study: Refrain

the perfect phrase
would draw out the colors
like water on stone

without shriveling fingertips
or making the feeling
too slippery to handle

the perfect phrase
a laminate, would
protect experience

from further tampering
while leaving its texture
in full view.

the perfect phrase
would come out right
the first time and dissipate

as quickly; from liquid
to solid to sublime—
instant as dew.

the perfect phrase
could stand divorced
from craft or insight

as only a form of breath
as only a relic of ink
before the self or text, in situ.

en hro, starched cloth,
ironed and stacked, extractable
expandable, collapsed as it

lineates the conditions
for its own unfolding.
the spirit finding material:

loose weave set in plaster
there is no motive here.
the perfect phrase:

a juxtaposition
beyond ethics,

light refracting through
a thick oiled lens.

at last night’s party at the moment
they decided it was their turn to leave

the floor is made into crickets
at first their chirping disparate
and then, warming each other
by song, they sync.

I come to you without nylon or lace
Without purple, void of red.
I come to you and my bed is on the floor
Lousy with papers, the screen lighting us
While the windows crinkle in this sad distance.

I come to you and I unload the stones
And foreign coins from my shoes.
I come to you as a small thing
I say to the dark, out from under
A sheet of concrete thin as bible paper.
I come to you and I forget theory
I come to you with gazes packed over my teeth.

I move patiently, I am quiet as an earthworm
I come to you, and I point at the sky, confuse objects for other images,
I forget, I practice

With you, I was convinced I could feel the earth moving beneath me
as a ball of oscillating strings. At first in the feet, then in the hand, then in the mouth.
Iced lake, sun split, minor pink. Fixed on the yellow home on the other side of our shared shore. Crossing, the time unaccounted, laying our palms —

The roof scintillating, verglas spilling into a cup on the frozen surface of the lake. An icicle calling, the long iridescent antenna of droplets. Faint splatter around the rim. How many cups have sunk this way?

—Stigmata reception. Wool having replaced the hollows of my hands. Wool having replaced the swallow of my mouth. The opposite of dialogue, oh, the way a hand makes spring: harmonic motion.

We fell sleep and left the microphones on

The absence of the previous loudness made more tangible
by the over amplification of small frictions in the dark.

In the dream, I slept inside a sac of heaven with you
when love passed between us as citrus, smoke, gesture,
becoming trapped by the event field of summer.
Our astral pouch among many in a cathedral.
Our voices, phrases built in parallel, casting prisms on the ceiling of the sanctuary, ripples formed in the stained glass.
Hinges, bergamot, palms, pickled ginger, our angled bodies in our velvet vault. Our breath running close to the limit of music. We climbed up the stairs of the bell tower, and exited the church through an oven, past a cake. (Now, as I retell this dream, many years later, I am still sorry. After some thinking, I am sorry that you were not the great love I took you for, that I would have given you the time remaining for me. Not only the structural time, but the experiential time too. I have to intellectualize it, or I’m still [...] That I pressed the point.)
When I listen back to the recording, just the faint hum of the needle
skipping across the cliff between the vinyl and the paper.
